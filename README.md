## 📖 About MSMS

The **Motorpool Services Management System (MSMS)** is an integrated platform designed for the DNSC Executive Affairs Division to enhance efficiency, transparency, and organization of vehicle services.

By transitioning from manual documentation to a **digital-first approach**, MSMS eliminates errors in:
- Trip logging
- Fuel monitoring
- Maintenance scheduling

The system ensures accountability through:
- GPS real-time tracking  
- Barcode-validated trip ticket system  

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📍 GPS & Real-time Tracking

- **Live Fleet Monitoring** – Real-time location tracking of vehicles and drivers  
- **Route Validation** – Ensures travel is conducted strictly on authorized routes  
- **Historical Playback** – Review previous trip paths and movements  

---

### 🎫 Smart Trip Authorization

- **Electronic Requests** – Advisors and coordinators can submit digital trip requests  
- **Barcode Validation** – Auto-generated trip tickets with unique barcodes  
- **Automated Approval Workflow** – Streamlined admin review process  

---

### ⛽ Resource & Maintenance Management

- **Fuel Efficiency Analytics** – Calculates fuel consumption based on distance  
- **Status Monitoring** – Tracks vehicle states:
  - Available  
  - On Travel  
  - Under Maintenance  
- **Service Logs** – Repair history + upcoming maintenance schedules  

---

### 📊 Reporting & Accountability

- **Electronic Trip Reports** – Auto-generated summaries (duration, driver, fuel)  
- **Management Dashboard** – Insights for decision-making  

---

## 👥 User Roles & Permissions

### 🛠 Administrator / Motorpool Director
- Approve requests  
- Monitor real-time GPS  
- Manage fleet records  
- Generate audit reports  

### 🚗 Driver
- Validate trips via barcode  
- Record departure/arrival  
- Report fuel & vehicle status  

### 📋 Advisor / Proctor Coordinator
- Submit vehicle requests  
- Track approval status  

### 👨‍🎓 Passenger (Students/Staff)
- Logged for attendance and safety verification
